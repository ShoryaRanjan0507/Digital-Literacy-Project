Social Media Checklist:-

Do's:
- Do regular privacy audits. Check who can see your posts and tagged photos every few months.
- Do curate a Professional Persona on platforms like LinkedIn to highlight your technical projects and skills.
- Do verify information before sharing or reposting. Check sources to avoid spreading hallucinations or fake news.
- Do use social media to follow industry leaders and companies you admire to stay updated on trends.
- Do think before you post. Ask yourself: "Would I be okay with a future recruiter seeing this during a background check?"

Don'ts:
- Don't share your real-time location. Wait until you’ve left a venue or event before posting about it.
- Don't post content that could be seen as unprofessional, such as complaints about professors, peers, or past employers.
- Don't engage in doomscrolling or toxic online arguments. It’s a drain on your time and mental energy.
- Don't send generic, copy-paste connection requests. Always include a brief, personalized note if possible.
- Don't assume that "Private" or "Deleted" means gone. Digital content can be screenshotted or archived instantly.
